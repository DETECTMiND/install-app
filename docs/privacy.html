Privacy Policy

Last updated 6 June 2026 · Data controller: Anoop Kadan | University of Southampton

This policy explains how the DETECTMiND research app collects, uses, and protects data from participants. By installing and using the app, you agree to the practices described here.

1. What data is collected

Only the sensors enabled for your study cohort are collected. The full set of possible sensors:

  • App usage
    Which apps you open, start/end time, duration. Package name only — no in-app content.
  • Notifications
    App that posted a notification, time, title (if shown on the lock screen). No notification body content stored.
  • Battery
    Level, charging state, temperature, voltage. No identifiers.
  • Calls
    Direction (incoming, outgoing, missed), duration, time. Phone numbers are SHA-256 hashed before storage.
  • SMS
    Direction, time. Contact number and message body are SHA-256 hashed; raw content is never stored.
  • Location
    GPS coordinates, accuracy, speed. Collected per study config — sampling rate varies.
  • Ambient light
    Light sensor readings in lux. No identifiers.
  • Screen state
    On / off / locked / unlocked events. No content.
  • Gestures
    Taps, scrolls, long-presses — type and target app only. No on-screen content; OS-enforced via the accessibility privacy flag.
  • Survey responses (Ecological Momentary Assessment)
    Your answers to short scheduled questions. Linked to your participant ID only.

2. What is NOT collected

  • The content of messages, emails, or notifications
  • Phone numbers in plain text (only hashed)
  • What you type, see, or hear on the screen
  • Audio, video, photos, or microphone input
  • Browsing history or web content
  • Your real name or contact details (only your participant ID)

3. How data is stored and transmitted

  • All data is first stored locally on your device's encrypted app storage.
  • Data is uploaded over HTTPS (TLS 1.2+) to a research database hosted by Supabase Inc. on EU servers.
  • Each upload is associated only with your study participant ID (a random UUID); your real identity is held separately by the research team in line with university data-management procedures.

4. Banking-app pause feature

Some banking apps refuse to open when an accessibility service is active. DETECTMiND detects when you launch a configured banking app and prompts you (via notification) to pause gesture collection. We do not collect or transmit any data about your banking activity itself.

5. Security

The Android app stores data locally on your device using Android's standard app-private storage, encrypted at rest by the OS. Network transmission uses TLS 1.2 or higher. The server uses row-level security to ensure only the research team can read participant data.

6. Children

This app is not intended for use by anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.
